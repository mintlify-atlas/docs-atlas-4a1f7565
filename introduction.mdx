Seerr is a free, open-source application that lets your users request movies and TV shows directly from your media server. It connects to Jellyfin, Plex, or Emby for authentication and library management, and routes approved requests automatically to Radarr and Sonarr for downloading.

## How Seerr works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Seerr">
    Deploy Seerr using Docker, Kubernetes, or from source. Access the web UI at `http://[your-server]:5055` to complete setup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your media server">
    Sign in with your Jellyfin, Plex, or Emby admin account and import your existing users. Seerr syncs your library so it knows what you already have.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Radarr and Sonarr">
    Point Seerr at your Radarr and Sonarr instances. When you approve a request, Seerr forwards it automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start accepting requests">
    Share Seerr with your users. They can browse, discover, and request media — and you can approve or auto-approve from any device.
  </Step>
</Steps>
